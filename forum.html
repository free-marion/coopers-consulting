What is the
Focus Forum?

Focus Forum is a two day event built for owners and leaders who carry the responsibility of growing organizations. If you possess the will to grow, but are struggling with the way, this conference is for you.

This is not a large scale, anonymous event. The Focus Forum began as a small group of business owners who gathered to learn the principles of the Entrepreneurial Operating System and discuss organizational issues amongst peers. This grew into our first gathering in St Louis Missouri in the summer of 2025. The 2 day conference was so successful that we immediately began planning for our second, which you are cordially invited to.

We do not aim to shallowly inspire, nor hide from uncomfortable truths. Sessions are practical, and the discussions are candid. Be prepared to set ego to the side.

The Conference

Connect with people who share your struggles, and receive inspiration from unexpected corners of the room.

Hosted in St. Louis and sponsored by Cooper's Consulting, Focus Forum reflects the same principles that drive our other ventures: clarity of vision, disciplined execution, and personal responsibility. Every element of the conference is built to respect your time and deliver substance.

We look forward to seeing you in June.
