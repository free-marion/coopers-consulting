What We Are

My job is not to
tell you who to be.

It's to help you discover your true purpose — and to remove every obstacle standing between you and it. NOBA is a screened, curated gathering of men who are done drifting and ready to build.


We work on the full arc: a 10-year picture, a 1-year plan, 90-day rocks, and the weekly habits that close the gap between who you are and who you're meant to be. You leave with a clear vision, a real plan, and a group of men who will hold you to it.

"If you don't know your WHY, your WHAT, where you're headed — NOBA can help, in a practical way."

— Derek McMinn, NOBA Member

The Gathering

One Weekend.
Every April.

We meet annually at Eagle Sky Camp of the Ozarks — 5,000 acres of Missouri wilderness that sets the tone before a word is spoken. No distractions. No noise. Just men doing the work.

The Venue

Eagle Sky Camp
of the Ozarks

We don't pick venues for the brochure. We pick them for what they do to a man the moment he arrives. Five thousand acres of Ozark terrain — lake, timber, ridge line — has a way of putting things in perspective before the weekend even begins.


Eagle Sky has hosted us since year one and the relationship has grown into something we're proud of. Their commitment to transformation, to getting people out of their heads and into something real, aligns with everything NOBA is about.
