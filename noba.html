What We Are

Not a retreat.
Not a conference.
An expedition into who you could be.

NOBA is three days in the Ozark wilderness where men who are serious about growth trade comfort for clarity. No speakers on a stage. No breakout sessions. Just rugged terrain, honest conversation, and the kind of structured challenge that strips away everything that isn't essential.

We screen every attendee. Not to be exclusive — to protect the environment. Because the work we do here only works when every man in the room is ready to do it. You don't come to NOBA to relax. You come to become a more powerful version of yourself.

The Framework

Three Forces. One Weekend.

01
THE WILD

Eagle Sky Camp sits in the Ozark Highlands — ridge trails, creek crossings, open sky at night. The setting isn't backdrop. It's the curriculum. Nature resets something in men that comfort has been slowly deleting.

02
THE WORK

Structured sessions on vision, leadership, accountability, and the hard questions men rarely get a room to ask. We use proven frameworks because discipline isn't the enemy of freedom — it's the only path to it.

03
THE PACK

The men you meet here become part of your permanent record. Shared adversity creates bonds that survive the drive home. NOBA alumni show up for each other in ways that have nothing to do with camp.
