002 — SETTING

Eagle Sky Camp sits above the treeline in the Missouri Ozarks. No signal. No distraction. Elevated ground. You arrive carrying whatever you've been avoiding.

No Boys Allowed is not a slogan. It's a standard. This is the second year we've gathered at Eagle Sky Camp in the Ozarks. Older men. Younger men. A weekend in the woods built around one question: what do you actually believe, and are you living it?
003 — principles passed down
The older men in this room have buried businesses and rebuilt them.

They're not here to inspire you.
They're here to tell you the truth.
