About

Dale Cooper& his son, Joel

The Work

We bring a unique lens to how organizations operate and improve. Our perspective is shaped by a mix of experience across generations, but more importantly by what has been built, tested, and refined in real businesses. Dale's work in the waterslide restoration industry, along with his recent acquisition of a golf course, led to the development of a disciplined R&D process focused on how organizations actually function.

The Method

Ideas were tested against reality. What failed was removed. What worked was kept and strengthened. The result is a practical set of tools that can be applied, adapted, and scaled.

The Principles

Our approach is grounded in timeless principles such as integrity, humility, and personal responsibility. At the same time, we stay current with the rapid changes driven by AI and emerging technologies. To see how this extends into digital systems and capabilities, visit our partner company, Magus Jane.
